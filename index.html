Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving technological advancements to foster creativity, entrepreneurship, and a knowledge-based economy in Bangladesh.

Sustainability

Promoting green energy, environmental preservation, and sustainable development to ensure a better future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient, and inclusive infrastructure to support economic growth and improve quality of life.

Empowerment

Focusing on education, skill development, and equal opportunities to empower citizens and drive social progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Our vision for 'Bangladesh 2.0' is ambitious yet grounded in the values of innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. It is about transforming challenges into opportunities, building modern infrastructure that serves all citizens, and fostering a spirit of creativity that drives economic progress. We aim to empower individuals through education and skill development, while ensuring that growth is inclusive, equitable, and environmentally sustainable. This vision is a call to action for every citizen to play their part in creating a brighter, more resilient future for Bangladesh."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

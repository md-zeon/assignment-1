Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving technological advancements to foster creativity, entrepreneurship, and a knowledge-based economy in Bangladesh.

Promoting green energy, environmental preservation, and sustainable development to ensure a better future for generations to come.

Infrastructure

Building modern, resilient, and inclusive infrastructure to support economic growth and improve quality of life.

Focusing on education, skill development, and equal opportunities to empower citizens and drive social progress.
